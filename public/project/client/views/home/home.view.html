Decision Making Strategies

Decision-making is hard. Sometimes we could use a little help...

 
Intuition Method
If you can't trust your own intuition, but you can trust your friends, try the Intuition Method to determine your best option. Also a great tool for making group decisions. You list the options and then poll your friends to decide.
 
Pro Con Method
The Weighted Pro/Con Method is a great tool if you have a yes/no question that you just can't figure out. If you still aren't confident after calculating your decision, poll your friends to get their opinions as well.
 
Grid Method
Coming soon is the Grid Method. This method is best used for complex decisions involving multiple options.
